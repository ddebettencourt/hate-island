What is Hate Island?

Many game shows revolve around love, what some might call the most basic of human desires. But we think there's another desire that might be even greater: the desire to be a hater.

The Experience

  • Hate Island takes place over the course of a weekend.
  • At the beginning of the weekend, you'll be assigned someone to hate.
  • Throughout, we'll have challenges and games where you aim to not only win, but also make the person you hate lose.
  • But you can't let them find out who you are! Subtly mess with them in a deniable way.
  • The winner will be crowned the "Biggest Hater".

Who Can Apply?

We're looking for people who:

  • consider themselves "haters"
  • are not looking for love
  • find most game show competitors annoying
  • are okay with not having a cool prize at the end
